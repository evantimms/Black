    • About Black

    Black is built and maintained by a single student. The idea behind Black was to create a easy way of managing my online spending. We live in a world today were our credit cards are stored on all different kinds of websites. It has become easier than ever to spend money online, and websites constantly battle with eachother for our attention, making the internet into one of the most advertistment heavy places. Black helps by providing a simple, easy to use budget that lets its users feel better when shopping online.
